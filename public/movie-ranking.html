The Best Movies According to Kristopher

My top 3 Movies of All Time

Training Day

Amazing movie starring Denzel Washington and Ethan Hawke which takes place in los Angeles back in 2001. This movie is excellent as it contrasts a newer officer joining the force and a veteran officer whom have been corrupted from the job.

Batman Dark Knight

Excellent DC Comics movie from 2008 that showcases Batman and his rouge's gallery.

Batman Beyond

One movie I've seen a million times as kid. This was gifted to me from my uncle and ranks as one of my favorites ever.
